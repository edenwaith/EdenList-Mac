Locating a font on your computer

You can use Font Book to quickly locate a font on your computer.

  1. Double-click Font Book in the Finder, or choose Manage Fonts from the Action pop-up menu in the lower-left corner of the Font panel.
  2. Select a font in the Font column and choose File > Show Font Files.

A new Finder window opens showing the font. Now you can quickly delete a duplicate font or relocate it.
